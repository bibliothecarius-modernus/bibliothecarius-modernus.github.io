About This Translation

This translation is part of the Bibliothecarius Modernus project, which uses AI technology to make Latin Patristic texts more accessible to audiences beyond specialized Latin scholarship. The translation prioritizes clarity and readability while attempting to preserve the original meaning.

The parallel presentation of Latin and English texts is designed to provide transparency and allow readers to verify the translation.
